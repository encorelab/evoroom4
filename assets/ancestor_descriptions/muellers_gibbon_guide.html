The Mueller's Gibbon monkey is an endangered monkey living in Brunei Durussalam, malaysia and indonesia. The population is estimated to be 250 000 but is rapidly decreasing. They can live over 40 years in captivity. They weigh 11.5 to 12.5 pounds and stand 1 and a half to 2 feet tall. They live in tropical rainforests and eat fruit, leaves and some invertebrates.
