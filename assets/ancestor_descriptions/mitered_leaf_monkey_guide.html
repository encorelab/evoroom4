The mitered leaf monkey has an undeveloped or absent brow ridges, ringed eyes and with prminent nasal bones and a sprout of hair on crest of head. Color varies from orange to black to white. The monkey typically weigh 5.8 to 7.4 kilos and stands 42 to59 cm tall. Theu are found on the island of Sumatra and another island just off the coast of Sumatra. They normally live in lowlying rainforests near rivers but have been known to be found in high elevation rainforests. The miltered leaf monkey is listed as endangered.
