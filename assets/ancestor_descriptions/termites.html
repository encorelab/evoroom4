Termites are a group of the order Blattodea of eusocial insects that eat decaying plant matter. These insects live in a caste structure typical to social insects, with specific insects for soldiers, workers and queens. Termites live in a range of tropical and subtropical regions. Termites often construct massive mounds in which they live, the largest known mounds being around 9m tall. The average however is closer to 3m tall.
